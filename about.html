About:

Howdy! This website is a collaboration of writer Timothy Stolkenheimer and editor Nikki M. to bring awareness to the Shminian cause.

Who are the Shmines?
The Shmines are a group of bird people from the planet Ruquosquak. If you want to know more, we recommend staring at the Origin section of this site.

Besides Shminian history, physiology, ecology, and linguistics, we do our part to study Shminian culture. You'll find plenty of overlap between human and Shminian sensibilities, so even though they come from a world bajillions of lightyears away, we hope you'll find these fancy alien critters aren't too different from us humans!
