Games:

Recently on my trip to Shmineland I picked up the game Rise of the Dead HD (the HD remake of a beautiful game). In this game you travel across Ruquosquak defending yourself from the undead and learning the secrets behind the chemical war that led to the outbreak. The remake also includes all of the DLC that went along with the game. This includes: Mission: Blizziard Island!, Retake Cannon Island, and Journey to the Moons of Ruquosquak!. The game also includes up to 8 players fighting against hordes of the undead over the internet.
