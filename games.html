Have you heard the good news??

Recently, our Editor Nikki was able to get ahold of two packs from the recent Shmine: The Gathering set Shmines of Ruquosquak. If you aren't familiar with our Earthly equivalent Magic: The Gathering, it's a card game where you duke it out with all sorts of creatures, sorceries, and enchantments, all flavorfully crafted to tell the story found in Magic's decades worth of lore. This story is released in small sets of like-themed cards.

In recent years, Wizards of the Coast has released a number of sets from well known IP's such as Lord of the Rings, Doctor Who, Warhammer 40k, and so on. What many of the most seasoned Magic players might not know is that Wizards have long since been releasing parallel sets through their subsidiary Shmines of the Coast, and as a result, our friends in Shmineland have been keeping pace with their own decades of unique lore and such for about as long as we have! In a similar vein to the Universes Beyond sets here, Shmines has been releasing more history-themed sets, like the one we're looking at today.

Shmines of Ruquosquak takes a dive into the history of the first true planet-wide Shminian conflict against the Schwabs. Many keen eyed players will recognize scenes like Duckard's Big Swipe, or the Betrayal of Awh'Gawban, in the backgrounds of some cards. This barely scratches the surface of historicity in this set, though.

In the coming weeks we'll be breaking down some of the deepest cuts in this set's lore, so keep an eye out, and in the meantime, take a gander at the Gallery for a look at some of the cards from this set!

Until next time,
-Timmy Stolkenheimer
